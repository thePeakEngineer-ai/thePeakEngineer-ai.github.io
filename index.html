Top Beginner-Friendly Trails

Perfect trails for those new to hiking in the Seattle area

Discovery Park

Seattle's largest park with stunning views of Puget Sound

  • 15 min from Seattle
  • 140 ft elevation gain
  • 2.8 miles loop
  • 1-1.5 hours

Snoqualmie Falls

Easy trail leading to one of Washington's most famous waterfalls

  • 35 min from Seattle
  • 250 ft elevation gain
  • 1.4 miles out & back
  • 45-60 minutes

Gold Creek Pond Loop

Scenic alpine lake with mountain views

  • 1 hour from Seattle
  • 45 ft elevation gain
  • 1.1 miles loop
  • 30-45 minutes

Franklin Falls

Family-friendly trail to a beautiful waterfall

  • 50 min from Seattle
  • 400 ft elevation gain
  • 2.0 miles out & back
  • 1-1.5 hours

Heybrook Lookout

Historic fire lookout with panoramic views

  • 1 hour 15 min from Seattle
  • 850 ft elevation gain
  • 2.6 miles out & back
  • 1.5-2 hours
